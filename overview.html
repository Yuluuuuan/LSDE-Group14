Description

How to evaluate the quality and value of an academic paper or scholar?

Whether some scholars frequently cite their own articles in order to increase their h-index?

When several authors always cite each other's literature?

How to define new metrics?

The purpose of this project is to process data of papers from Crossref:

(1) Calculate H-indexes of authors and compare them with those of Google Scholar.

(2) Find the most impactful papers in the last fifteen years.

(3) Set rank for authors according to their self-citations and inner-circle.

(4) Such results would be visualized.
